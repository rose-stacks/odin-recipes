Lemon Chicken

Lemon Chicken recipe with Zesty Flavour

Easy one pan whole chicken cooked in a tangy lemon sauce and fresh herbs.

Quick prep go to meal for a special occasion or your weekly meal prep.

Ingredients

  • 2 Whole Chickens
  • 3 Garlic cloves
  • 2 Lemons
  • 1 tsp Oregano
  • 1 Fresh Parsley
  • 1/2 cup Chicken Broth
  • 3 tbs Olive oil
  • 2 tbs unsalted Butter
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
